Job Specifications

As a valued member of our Web Development team, your responsibilities include collaborating with teammates to support the development of our clients' websites and other digital needs. You'll utilise a wide range of technologies and coding abilities to build functionality into websites while fostering teamwork and communication within the team.
What we are looking for:

  • Some knowledge in HTML, CSS and JavaScript
  • Willing to learn backend languages like PHP
  • Effective team player
  • Good problem-solving skills
  • The willingness to learn and adaptbily
  • Strong communication abilities

Requirements

You do not require much to become a Web Developer as long as you have some knowledge behind it. No formal degree is needed in this role as you will be aided by dedicated and experienced web developers in the team, which your communication skills will play a key aspect in your development.
What you will need:

Required

  • 5 or more GCSE's with C or above
  • English and Maths C or above
  • Computing subject level 3 or higher

Desirable

  • Bootcamps/Online Courses Certificates
  • Portfolio of projects
  • Use of Specialised Programs
